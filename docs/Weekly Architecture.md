# Weekly Architecture

*The daily routine runs every day. This is how the week is structured around it.*

---

## Sample Week (Normal)

| Day | AM Training (5-6:30 AM) | Zone 2 Bike | Evening |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Monday** | Bench Day (~80 min) | — | Supplements, cyclic sighing, wind down |
| **Tuesday** | — | Zone 2 ride (45-60 min) | Supplements, cyclic sighing, wind down |
| **Wednesday** | Squat Day (~80 min) | — | Supplements, cyclic sighing, wind down |
| **Thursday** | — | Zone 2 ride (45-60 min) | Supplements, cyclic sighing, wind down |
| **Friday** | Press Day (~80 min) | — | Supplements, cyclic sighing, wind down |
| **Saturday** | HIIT hill ride (~45 min) | — | Supplements, cyclic sighing, wind down |
| **Sunday** | Hinge Day (~70 min) | Zone 2 ride (45-60 min) | Meal prep, pill organizer refill, weekly review |

**Zone 2 total:** ~135-180 min (3 rides). Add a 4th ride on a lift day if under 180 min.

**Daily (every day):** Dog walk, TM meditation, morning sunlight, morning supplements with breakfast, evening supplements + cyclic sighing

## Deload Week (Every 4th Week)

| Day | AM Training | Zone 2 Bike | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Monday** | Bench Day (drop 1-2 sets, 70-75% intensity) | — | Same exercises, less volume |
| **Tuesday** | — | Zone 2 ride (45-60 min) | Keep Zone 2 volume the same |
| **Wednesday** | Squat Day (drop 1-2 sets, 70-75% intensity) | — | No PRs, focus on movement quality |
| **Thursday** | — | Zone 2 ride (45-60 min) | |
| **Friday** | Press Day (drop 1-2 sets, 70-75% intensity) | — | |
| **Saturday** | Zone 2 ride (45-60 min) | — | Replace HIIT with Zone 2 this week |
| **Sunday** | Hinge Day (drop 1-2 sets, 70-75% intensity) | — | Weekly review, assess recovery |

**Key:** Deload reduces lifting volume and intensity but maintains Zone 2 cardio. No HIIT during deload week. Sleep and recovery are the priority — this is where hormones and adaptations catch up.

---

## Sunday Weekly Review (10 min)

1. **Training:** Did all 4 lifts happen? Did any lifts go up? Any sessions missed?
2. **Cardio:** Hit 180 min Zone 2? HIIT session feel strong?
3. **Recovery:** Whoop recovery trending green? Any persistent fatigue or soreness?
4. **Nutrition:** Hitting 160g protein daily? Fermented foods happening?
5. **Sleep:** Averaging 8 hours? Any nights under 7?
6. **One thing to fix next week**
7. **Refill pill organizer + check supplement inventory**

## Ashwagandha Cycling

Weeks 1-4: Take 600mg nightly
Week 5: Off — drop ashwagandha, keep everything else
Weeks 6-9: Back on
Week 10: Off

*Mark the off-weeks on your calendar or set a recurring reminder.*

## Monthly Check-In

- **Week 4 (deload):** Progress photos (same lighting, same time). Compare lifts to last month. Review Whoop trends. Adjust anything that isn't working.
- **Quarterly:** Retest bloodwork (hormonal + metabolic markers). Compare to baseline. Reassess supplement stack — is everything validated by the data?
