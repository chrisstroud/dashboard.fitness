## Hypertrophy Programs

### Progression Paradigm (2024)
1. Rep progression first — increase reps at same weight until hitting target range
2. Target range — 3x5–8 working up to 3x12–15 before adding weight
3. MYO sets for plateaus and extra volume
4. Add weight only when form is locked in across all reps
5. Session structure — first 30 min: 5–10 reps · last 30 min: supersets 10–15 reps

## Track & Sprint Protocol

*2x/week. Speed, endurance, and overall athleticism.*

1. **Warm-Up (10 min)** — light jog + dynamic stretches (leg swings, arm circles, lunges)
2. **Sprint Drills (10 min)** — high knees 30s x3 · butt kicks 30s x3 · strides 50–60m x4
3. **Main Set** — 100m @ 75–90% effort · walk back for full recovery · 8–10 reps
4. **Cool Down (10 min)** — light jog + static stretching (legs and hips)

*Notes: hydrate before/during/after · maintain form throughout · minimum 1 rest day between sprint sessions*
