#### Blood Work to Track
**Priority — get baseline draw (morning, before 10 AM):**
- Cardiovascular: ApoB (target < 60), Lp(a) (one-time genetic test), lipid panel
- Hormonal: Total T, free T, SHBG, estradiol, DHEA-S, prolactin, IGF-1, AM cortisol
- Thyroid: TSH, free T3, free T4, reverse T3, TPO antibodies
- Metabolic: Fasting insulin (target < 6), fasting glucose, HbA1c (target < 5.2%), HOMA-IR (target < 1.0)
- Inflammatory: hsCRP (target < 0.5), homocysteine
- Nutrients: 25-hydroxyvitamin D (target 40–60 ng/mL), ferritin, RBC magnesium
- Other: CBC, comprehensive metabolic panel
- **Note:** 10g creatine will elevate creatinine/eGFR — request cystatin C if flagged

**Where:** Ulta Lab Tests or Marek Health (~$420-620). Morning draw, fasted.

**Cadence:** Baseline now → retest hormonal + metabolic at 3 months → annual comprehensive
