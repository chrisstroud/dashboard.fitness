## Training Ethos

> *Be disciplined but enjoy and have fun with training. Train pain free. Come out of workouts feeling good. Full ROM and good technique and control. Train everyday — otherwise the mind rebels. And if the mind rebels, the body will follow.*

**Conjugate principle:** Always be working on strength, hypertrophy, and endurance simultaneously. Well-rounded strength is the goal.

**Training purpose:** Be athletic. Training geared toward serving home life, outdoors, mental/physical state, and travel. Through-line is athleticism — not aesthetics, not numbers for their own sake.

**Talk track:** Train every day, but most days is also acceptable. Standard session is 90 minutes; 60 min is fine. Some big days with double or triple sessions. VO2 (Zone 2/5), strength, and dog walking distributed throughout the week. Sports and outdoors as much as possible — tennis with Kelsey.

**If anyone asks:** "Just a mix of weights, biking, and lots of dog walking." 😄

**Athletic things I like:** ATG · Dumbbells · Barbells · Running · Biking · Mobility · Stretching · Yoga wheel

**My body:** Needs to know it's going to work today. Otherwise it rebels.
