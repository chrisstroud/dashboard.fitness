# Testosterone Maxxing

**6-Week Transformation: Turning 40 at Full Power**

Start: March 20, 2026 | Birthday: April 29, 2026

---

## The Daily Protocol

This is the whole program. Do this every day.

### 5:00 AM — Wake Up

- Immediate rise, no snooze
- Cold water on face
- Black coffee

### 5:10 AM — Train

- **Mon / Wed / Fri:** Lift (your program — heavy compounds, progressive overload)
- **Tue / Sat:** Hill ride (~30 min — 20 min uphill hard, 10 min downhill easy)
- **Thu / Sun:** Walk, stretch, or rest

### 6:00 AM — Sunlight

- 10-20 min outside within 60 min of waking
- Face toward sky, no sunglasses
- Cloudy days still count — go outside anyway

### 6:20 AM — Cold Shower

- Week 1: 30 sec → Week 2: 1 min → Week 3+: 2-3 min
- Not for testosterone directly — for discipline, dopamine, and norepinephrine
- **Never cold plunge right after lifting** — blunts hormonal response. Mornings only.

### 6:30 AM — Morning Supplements

Take with coffee or first meal, whichever comes first.

| Supplement | Dose | Why |
|------------|------|-----|
| Vitamin D3 + K2 | 5,000 IU D3 / 200mcg K2 | Directly correlated with T levels |
| Zinc | 15-20mg (picolinate) | Required for T synthesis, depleted by sweat |
| Boron | 10mg | Frees bound testosterone (+28% free T in studies) |
| Fish Oil | 3-5g EPA/DHA | Anti-inflammatory |
| Creatine | 5g monohydrate | Strength, cognition, well-proven |

### 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM — First Meal

- 3-4 whole eggs, grass-fed butter
- Protein shake: whole milk + 50g whey + creatine (if not taken earlier)
- Oats with cinnamon + raw honey
- 1/2 avocado

### ~3:00 PM — Second Meal

- 6-8 oz beef, salmon, or chicken thighs
- Big serving of cruciferous vegetables (broccoli, cauliflower, Brussels sprouts)
- Sweet potato or rice

### 6:00 - 8:00 PM — Last Meal

- 6-8 oz protein (salmon, steak, chicken)
- Massive vegetable plate (spinach, kale, peppers, asparagus)
- Olive oil drizzle
- 2-3 Brazil nuts

### 8:00 PM — Wind Down

- Dim lights, no screens (or blue-blocking glasses)
- No news, no work

### 8:30 PM — Evening Supplements

| Supplement | Dose | Why |
|------------|------|-----|
| Magnesium Glycinate | 400mg | Sleep quality, T production, recovery |
| Ashwagandha KSM-66 | 600mg | Reduces cortisol 30%, increases T 15-17% |

### 9:00 PM — Bed

- Phone in another room
- Bedroom at 65-68°F, complete blackout
- Target 8 hours — sleep is the #1 testosterone lever

## The Rules

1. **Sleep 8 hours** — poor sleep drops testosterone 10-15% in one week
2. **Hit 160g protein** — minimum, every day
3. **No alcohol** — even 2-3 drinks = 7% T drop + wrecked sleep
4. **No caffeine after noon** — half-life is 5-6 hours
5. **Train fasted, eat big when you break** — first meal is protein-heavy
6. **Don't overdo it** — 3 lift days, 2 rides. Overtraining spikes cortisol which kills T
7. **Cook at home** — avoid seed oils, processed food, plastic containers

---

## What to Avoid

- **Alcohol** — full stop for 6 weeks
- **Processed sugar** — 25% acute T decrease
- **Seed oils** (canola, soybean, vegetable) — olive oil, butter, coconut oil instead
- **Plastic food containers** — BPA/phthalates are endocrine disruptors. Use glass.
- **Spearmint and licorice** — both actively lower testosterone in studies
- **Aggressive calorie cutting** — deficit >500 cal/day suppresses T production. Eat enough.
- **Soy protein isolate** in large amounts
- **Fragranced products** — parabens and phthalates. Switch to fragrance-free.

**Daily (2 minutes):** Weight (morning, post-bathroom). Energy 1-10. Sleep 1-10.

## Overtraining Warning Signs

If you see these, take 3-5 days off:
- Strength declining over 2+ weeks despite good sleep
- Persistent fatigue that rest doesn't fix
- Resting heart rate up 5-10 BPM for multiple days
- Loss of morning erections

More sessions ≠ more results. Recovery is where the hormones do their work.

## Key Research Notes

- **Sleep is #1.** Testosterone is produced during deep sleep. Everything else is secondary.
- **Cold showers** are for discipline and dopamine (300% norepinephrine spike), not direct T increase.
- **16:8 fasting** may lower T in trained men — monitor and shorten to 14:10 if energy/libido dips.
- **Creatine** is safe. The DHT concern was debunked by a 2025 RCT.
- **Ashwagandha** reaches efficacy around week 4. Be patient.
- **Sauna/heat** is great but time it 4+ hours after lifting, not immediately after.
- **Realistic 6-week outcome:** 10-25% T increase, 5-15% strength gains, better sleep/energy/mood. Visible body recomp is subtle — the real payoff is setting up weeks 7-12.
