# Training Program

*75-90 min sessions. Don't rush the warm-up. Don't rush the rest. Don't skip core.*

## Strength @4x/week

- [Bench Day](Bench%20Day.md) — Bench, incline, rows, shoulders, arms @75min
- [Squat Day](Squat%20Day.md) — Back squat, Bulgarians, weighted chins @80min
- [Press Day](Press%20Day.md) — OHP, shoulders, arms, back extensions @80min
- [Hinge Day](Hinge%20Day.md) — Deadlift/RDL, glutes, hamstrings, rows @70min

## Cardio @5x/week

- [Cardio](Cardio.md) — 1x HIIT hill ride + 3-4x Zone 2 bike rides (target 180 min/week Zone 2)
- HIIT hill ride @1x/week @45min @calendar
- Zone 2 bike ride @4x/week @50min @calendar

## Daily

- TM meditation @2x/day @20min @calendar
- Dog walk @daily @reminder
- Cyclic sighing @daily @evening @5min @reminder

## Deload @cycle(3on/1off)

- Every 4th week: same exercises, drop 1-2 sets, reduce intensity to 70-75%. Prioritize sleep and recovery.
- Use Whoop recovery: green = push, yellow = cap RPE at 7-8, red = active recovery only

## Design Principles

- DNS stability (dead bugs, bird dogs, crocodile breathing) → ATG prehab → lift. Every session.
- Core finisher on every lifting day (5 min)
- Shoulders hit on both upper days
- Upper pull on both lower days (chins on squat, rows on hinge)
- Explosive work before both lower days
