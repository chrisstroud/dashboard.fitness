# Cardio

## Current

**Target: 180+ min Zone 2 per week + 1 HIIT session**

**1x/week — HIIT hill ride:** Norwegian 4x4. 4 min hard (85-95% HRmax) / 3 min easy (~70% HRmax) x4. ~45 min with warm-up/cooldown. Use Whoop to track HR zones.

**3-4x/week — Zone 2 bike rides:** 45-60 min each, easy pace, conversational. Talk test: can converse but not sing. Accumulate 180 min/week total. Can be outdoor rides or stationary.

**Why 180 min:** Attia's minimum effective dose for Zone 2. Below this, cardiovascular and metabolic adaptations are limited. This volume also supports cognitive health (BDNF), fat oxidation, and cardiac remodeling. 60 min/week was the previous target — research showed this was only 33% of the recommended dose.
